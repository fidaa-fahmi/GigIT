# GigIT User Guide

## Welcome to GigIT

GigIT is a hyperlocal gig-workforce platform built for Sabah students and local businesses.

Our mission is simple:

- Help students discover flexible nearby work opportunities
- Help businesses hire reliable local workers quickly
- Reduce no-shows through trust, ratings, and reliability tracking
- Build a trusted workforce ecosystem for Sabah

GigIT serves two user groups:

1. Workers (Students & Freelancers)
2. Employers (SMEs & Event Organizers)

---

# Getting Started

## Sign In

1. Launch GigIT.
2. Click **Google Sign-In**.
3. Select your Google account.
4. Complete authentication.
5. Your profile avatar will appear once successfully logged in.

---

# Worker Guide

## Worker Dashboard

The Worker Dashboard allows you to:

- Browse available gigs
- Track applications
- Monitor your Reliability Score
- View earnings
- Manage your profile

---

## Student Verification

Verified students gain additional trust and visibility.

### Steps

1. Open **Profile**
2. Select **Student Verification**
3. Upload your student identification card
4. Submit verification request

Once approved, a **Verified Student Badge** will appear on your profile.

---

## Reliability Score

Your Reliability Score measures your work reliability.

### Score Factors

| Action | Impact |
|----------|----------|
| Complete job on time | Positive |
| Receive good ratings | Positive |
| Late arrival | Negative |
| Last-minute cancellation | Negative |
| No-show | Major penalty |

Higher scores improve your chances of getting approved for future gigs.

---

## Browse Available Gigs

1. Open **Browse Gigs**
2. View available opportunities
3. Filter by:
   - Job Category
   - Location
   - Pay Rate
   - Date

Each gig card displays:

- Job title
- Employer
- Location
- Working hours
- Compensation
- Required workers

---

## Apply for a Gig

1. Open a gig listing
2. Review details carefully
3. Click **Apply**
4. Wait for employer approval

Application statuses:

- Pending
- Approved
- Rejected
- Completed

---

## Track Applications

Open **My Applications** to view:

- Pending applications
- Approved shifts
- Completed gigs
- Rejected applications

---

## Earnings & Payments

The Earnings page shows:

- Completed gig payments
- Payment status
- Payment history
- Total earnings

Workers can track payout progress directly from the dashboard.

---

## Travel Assistance

For selected jobs, travel support may be available.

Workers can:

- View eligibility
- Claim travel vouchers
- Track transportation benefits

---

## Job Completion & Ratings

After completing a gig:

1. Mark the shift as completed
2. Employer submits a rating
3. Reliability Score updates automatically

Strong ratings help build your reputation.

# Employer Guide

## Employer Dashboard

The Employer Dashboard helps businesses:

- Post jobs
- Review applicants
- Manage shifts
- Monitor worker reliability
- Access backup workers

---

## Create a Job Posting

1. Select **Create New Gig**
2. Enter:
   - Job Title
   - Description
   - Location
   - Shift Date
   - Working Hours
   - Payment Amount
   - Number of Workers Needed

3. Publish the posting

The gig will become visible to eligible workers.

---

## Review Applications

Open **Manage Applicants**.

You can review:

- Worker profile
- Reliability Score
- Verification badges
- Previous ratings

Actions available:

- Approve
- Reject
- Chat

---

## Communicate with Workers

Employers can use the built-in messaging feature to:

- Confirm shift details
- Clarify expectations
- Coordinate arrival times

---

## Manage Active Shifts

Track:

- Approved workers
- Upcoming shifts
- Attendance status
- Completed jobs

---

## Emergency Backup Pool

If an approved worker cancels:

1. Open **Emergency Backup Pool**
2. View standby workers nearby
3. Select replacement worker
4. Send backup request

This helps reduce operational disruptions.

---

## Rate Workers

After a shift:

1. Open completed job record
2. Submit worker rating
3. Leave optional feedback

Ratings contribute to worker Reliability Scores.

# Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I increase my Reliability Score?

Complete jobs on time, maintain good ratings, and avoid cancellations.

### Can I apply for multiple gigs?

Yes, as long as schedules do not conflict.

### What happens if I cancel a confirmed shift?

Your Reliability Score may decrease.

### How do employers identify reliable workers?

By reviewing Reliability Scores, verification badges, and previous ratings.

### How are backup workers selected?

Backup workers are chosen from available, pre-screened nearby candidates.
